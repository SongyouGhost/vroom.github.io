碳路 Road of Lux

A06 停格動畫

以「路」為發想，將煤炭擬人化為煤炭精靈，並以「閃亮的光」作為對比。在被規劃好的道路中，主角卻被一路上遇見的光所吸引，做出了與其他煤炭不同的選擇，走進了截然不同的岔路，儘管是漆黑、不起眼的存在，卻有努力追求的勇氣。而在追求的路途上經歷了波折與歷練，也讓漆黑的煤炭成為了閃閃發光的鑽石，成為了自己的光。

Inspired by "road," coal is personified as a coal spirit, contrasting with the "bright light." In the planned road, however, the protagonist is attracted by the light encountered along the way, making a different choice from other coals, entering a completely different path. Despite being a dark and inconspicuous existence, there is the courage to pursue. And through the twists and turns of the pursuit, the dark coal becomes a shining diamond, becoming its own light.
