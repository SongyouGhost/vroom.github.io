C06 地質公園伴手禮設計

探索臺灣十大地質公園，享用不同的酸甜口味。
‍
為臺灣十大地質公園做伴手禮設計，利用外觀及紋路看似岩石的蜜餞做設計，透過吃蜜餞觀察兩者造型上的巧合，認識臺灣地質公園的岩石與地景特色。

Explore Taiwan's top ten geological parks and enjoy a variety of sweet and sour flavors.

Designing souvenirs for Taiwan's top ten geological parks, utilizing dried fruits with appearances and patterns resembling stones for the design. Through consuming the dried fruits, one can observe the similarities in shape between them and the stones, thereby understanding the geological features of Taiwan's geological parks.
