(癌)and my Dear.
‍
推廣癌症患者與伴侶癒後親密關係的議題。癌症不應阻礙愛、溝通和互動，「I,Dear 癌症癒後蜜月推廣節」旨在探討罹癌者和伴侶如何重拾親密關係。
‍

(癌)and my Dear.

Promoting the topic of post-cancer intimacy between partners. Cancer should not hinder love, communication, and interaction. "I, Dear Cancer Post-Healing Honeymoon Promotion Festival" aims to explore how cancer patients and their partners can regain intimacy.

Through various health education activities and visual design, this project aims to correct misconceptions about post-cancer sexual life. The goal is to guide patients and partners to use methods such as speaking, listening, understanding, and companionship to make their love, communication, and sexual life happier.
