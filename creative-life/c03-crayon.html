CRayon

C03 化妝品循環設計-石頭蠟筆

｢CRayon｣是一款經淘汰化妝品再製的無毒蠟筆，主打安定、簡潔的淺色調，主要是提供自閉症兒童的輔助教材。除了給家長放心的選擇，也傳達消費者及孩童循環設計的價值

"CRayon" stone crayon is a non-toxic crayon that is remade from eliminated cosmetics. It focuses on stable and simple light colors. It is mainly used as an auxiliary teaching material for children with autism. In addition to giving parents a safe choice, it also conveys the value of circular design to consumers and children.
