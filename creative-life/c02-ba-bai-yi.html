八百藝

C02 台南百年技藝老店

除了談論古老府城的「技藝」，我們也應該停下腳步，細心觀察並捕捉塑造自己的「記憶」。

在2024年，台南迎來建城四百年的紀念，這座城市擁有豐富的歷史技藝。然而，許多傳統的技藝卻正在逐漸消失⋯⋯ 因此，我們挑選了八家百年老店，以包裝的方式展現它們各自獨特的特色。

In addition to discussing the ancient skills of the old capital city, let us also pause to observe and capture the shaping of our own memories.

In 2024, Tainan celebrates its 400th anniversary since its founding, boasting historical craftsmanship. However, many traditional skills are fading away. Therefore, we have selected eight century-old shops and presented their distinctive features through packaging.
